## Blocker classification (read first) — DECISION D-027

Per **DECISION D-027** (PROPOSED, accepted for the RF-004 freeze candidate; pending Saleh's approval, **NOT** frozen), open questions are classified for blocking as follows:

- **RF-004 human architecture approval gates the START of [M0B](PROJECT_PLAN.md) as a milestone.** It does **not** require all of **Q-001..Q-024** to be resolved first.
- **An individual open question blocks ONLY the tickets that depend on its answer** — not a whole milestone, and not foundation/monorepo/CI work that does not touch the unanswered area.
- A new status, **"Accepted Open"**, applies to a question that may remain open without blocking the M0B milestone, provided **all four** conditions hold:
  1. its **owner is assigned**;
  2. its **blocking ticket / milestone is identified**;
  3. a **safe interim interface / config-point / placeholder / feature-flag exists** so dependent scaffolding can proceed; and
  4. **no irreversible schema or contract assumption** is made on the unanswered point.

This register covers **Q-001..Q-024**. RF-004 does **not** require all of them resolved; each unresolved question blocks only its dependent tickets per the **Blocking scope** column below.

## Early-resolution priority (read first)

A subset of these questions is high-priority for early engineering work and is tracked under ticket RF-003. These gate **specific tickets**, not all of M0B; per **DECISION D-027**, the start of the M0B milestone is gated by **RF-004 human architecture approval**, not by these questions. Each item below may be marked **"Accepted Open"** (owner assigned + safe-interim condition per D-027) rather than treated as a hard milestone blocker:

- **Q-001 — Target country / jurisdiction.** Gates legal/tax/fiscal/privacy work and the freeze of tax behaviour in [MONEY_AND_TAX_SPEC](MONEY_AND_TAX_SPEC.md); it does **not** gate monorepo/CI/foundation tickets. May be **Accepted Open** with an owner while foundation work proceeds.
- **Q-007 — Default currency & single-vs-multi-currency.** Does **not** block generic money types using ISO 4217 currency codes (per **DECISION D-007**, money is integer minor units carrying a currency code); it gates the **default pilot currency configuration**. May be **Accepted Open** while the currency-agnostic money type is scaffolded.
- **Q-006 / Q-015 — Pilot hardware & printing connectivity/encoding.** Gate pilot hardware selection + M3 verification and printer implementation/goldens (Q-015); they do **not** gate the abstract printing **adapter interface** (de-risks Arabic/Hebrew printing — **RISK R-001**, **RISK R-006**). May be **Accepted Open** behind a replaceable printing adapter.
- **Q-008 — MFA method and mandatory roles.** Gates the concrete MFA provider/enforcement implementation (**DECISION D-006**); it does **not** gate the identity **interfaces**. May be **Accepted Open** behind those interfaces.
- **Q-009 — Offline authorization validity window.** Gates final revocation/session hardening (**DECISION D-010**; **RISK R-007**); it does **not** gate Drift outbox/inbox **local-store scaffolding**. May be **Accepted Open** with a placeholder/config-point window.

Per **DECISION D-027**, every question in the register is recorded as **Accepted Open** and blocks only its dependent tickets, not whole milestones. Nothing here blocks **M0A** (this milestone is documentation-only); the M0B milestone start is gated by **RF-004** approval, not by the items above.

## Lifecycle and cross-references

- **Closing a question:** record a **DECISION D-xxx** in [DECISIONS.md](DECISIONS.md), then update the row here to `Status: Resolved → D-xxx` and add a dated note. Documents that cited the `Q-xxx` should then cite the new `D-xxx`.
- **Adding a question:** allocate the next free ID (**Q-025+**; Q-018–Q-024 are already allocated), fill every column, and note the originating document. Never renumber existing IDs.
- **Markers:** This register uses **OPEN QUESTION** (every row), **ASSUMPTION** (non-binding leanings), **DEFERRED** (Q-011, Q-016, and contingent items), **DECISION** (cited decisions, frozen only after review and approval), and **RISK** (cited register IDs). The **Status** field may also be **Accepted Open** per **DECISION D-027** (open but non-blocking for the M0B milestone under the four conditions above). Do not hide unresolved issues as silent assumptions.
